# LinkedIn Outreach Templates — Cas Glavce

---

## Template 1: Cold Connection to CEO/President (Target Company)

**Use when:** You want to get on radar of a company you'd consider joining, even if no role is posted.

```
Hi [First Name],

Following your work at [Company] — particularly [one specific thing: recent expansion, acquisition, new market].

I'm a CCO with 20+ years in industrial distribution and a track record of scaling revenue to $100M+.
Exploring my next executive move in [industry].

Would love to connect.

— Cas
```

**Why it works:** Short, specific, zero-ask. They accept the connection; you follow up in 5 days.

## Response to Recruiter Who Reaches Out

```
[First Name], thank you — I'm open to the right conversation.

My priorities:
- Role: President, CCO, CRO, or VP Sales
- Industries: industrial distribution, HVAC, capital equipment, renewables, circular economy
- Location: GTA hybrid or North America remote
- Compensation: $300K+ total comp ($250K+ base)

Does this opportunity align with those parameters?
```
